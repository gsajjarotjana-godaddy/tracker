HOW TO OPEN IN SAFARI ON YOUR IPHONE:
========================================

When the HTML file opens in the Files app:

METHOD 1 (Easiest):
1. Long press (hold) on the index.html file
2. Tap "Share"
3. Scroll and tap "Safari" (or look for the Safari icon)
4. The app will open in Safari!

METHOD 2:
1. Tap the index.html file to preview it
2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow) at the top
3. Tap "Safari" from the share menu
4. The app will open in Safari!

METHOD 3 (Add to Home Screen):
1. Open the file in Safari (using Method 1 or 2)
2. Tap the Share button in Safari
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Now you can open it like a regular app!

IMPORTANT:
- Once opened in Safari, your data will be saved automatically
- The app works offline - no internet needed
- Your data stays on your phone and never resets
- You can bookmark it in Safari for easy access
