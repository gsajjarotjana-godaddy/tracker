EASIEST WAY TO OPEN ON YOUR IPHONE:
====================================

OPTION 1: EMAIL IT (RECOMMENDED - EASIEST!)
--------------------------------------------
1. On your Mac, right-click "index.html"
2. Choose "Share" → "Mail" (or attach to an email)
3. Email it to yourself
4. On your iPhone, open the email
5. Tap the "index.html" attachment
6. It will automatically open in Safari! ✅

This is the easiest method and always works!


OPTION 2: USE A SIMPLE WEB SERVER
----------------------------------
1. On your Mac, double-click "start-server.sh"
   (Or open Terminal and run: cd "/Users/grace/Desktop/development/habit tracker" && python3 -m http.server 8000)

2. Find your Mac's IP address:
   - Open System Settings → Network
   - Click on your WiFi connection
   - Look for the IP address (e.g., 192.168.1.100)

3. On your iPhone (same WiFi network):
   - Open Safari
   - Type in address bar: http://[YOUR-MAC-IP]:8000
   - Example: http://192.168.1.100:8000
   - Press Go
   - The app will open! ✅

4. To stop the server, press Ctrl+C in Terminal


OPTION 3: USE ICLOUD DRIVE
---------------------------
1. Upload "index.html" to iCloud Drive
2. On your iPhone, open Safari
3. Go to iCloud.com and sign in
4. Open iCloud Drive
5. Tap "index.html"
6. It will open in Safari! ✅


WHICH METHOD TO USE?
- Option 1 (Email) is the EASIEST and most reliable
- Option 2 (Server) is good if you want to access it anytime
- Option 3 (iCloud) works but requires internet

Once it's open in Safari, you can:
- Add it to Home Screen (Share → Add to Home Screen)
- Bookmark it for easy access
- Your data will save automatically!
