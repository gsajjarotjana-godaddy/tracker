SAFARI ISN'T SHOWING? HERE ARE WORKAROUNDS:
============================================

METHOD 1: EMAIL IT TO YOURSELF (EASIEST)
-----------------------------------------
1. On your Mac, right-click index.html
2. Choose "Share" → "Mail" (or attach to email)
3. Email it to yourself
4. On your iPhone, open the email
5. Tap the HTML attachment
6. It will open in Safari automatically!


METHOD 2: USE SAFARI'S DOWNLOAD MANAGER
-----------------------------------------
1. Upload index.html to iCloud Drive (or any cloud service)
2. On your iPhone, open Safari
3. Go to iCloud.com and sign in
4. Open iCloud Drive
5. Tap the index.html file
6. It will open in Safari!


METHOD 3: USE A SIMPLE WEB SERVER
----------------------------------
1. On your Mac, open Terminal
2. Navigate to this folder:
   cd "/Users/grace/Desktop/development/habit tracker"
3. Run this command:
   python3 -m http.server 8000
4. On your iPhone (same WiFi network):
   Open Safari and go to: http://[YOUR-MAC-IP]:8000
   (Find your Mac's IP: System Settings → Network)


METHOD 4: USE THE "OPEN IN..." OPTION
--------------------------------------
1. In Files app, long-press index.html
2. Look for "Open in..." or "Open with..."
3. If you see a browser option, use that
4. Or try "Copy" then paste the file path in Safari's address bar


METHOD 5: USE A BOOKMARKLET (ADVANCED)
--------------------------------------
I can create a special bookmark you can use - just ask!


RECOMMENDED: METHOD 1 (Email) is the easiest and most reliable!
