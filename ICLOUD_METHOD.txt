✅ BEST METHOD: USE iCLOUD DRIVE (GUARANTEED TO WORK!)
=======================================================

This is the MOST RELIABLE way to open HTML files in Safari on iPhone:

STEP 1: UPLOAD TO iCLOUD DRIVE
-------------------------------
1. On your Mac, open Finder
2. Press Cmd+Shift+I (or Go → iCloud Drive)
3. Create a new folder called "HabitTracker" (optional)
4. Drag "index.html" into iCloud Drive
5. Wait for it to sync (you'll see a cloud icon)


STEP 2: OPEN ON YOUR IPHONE
----------------------------
1. On your iPhone, open Safari (NOT the Files app!)
2. Go to: iCloud.com
3. Sign in with your Apple ID
4. Tap "iCloud Drive"
5. Find and tap "index.html"
6. It will open in Safari! ✅


STEP 3: ADD TO HOME SCREEN
---------------------------
1. Once it's open in Safari, tap the Share button
2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
3. Name it "Habit Tracker"
4. Tap "Add"
5. Now you have an app icon on your home screen!


WHY THIS WORKS:
--------------
- iCloud.com opens files directly in Safari
- No server needed
- Works from anywhere (not just same WiFi)
- Your data saves in Safari's localStorage
- Works offline after first load


IMPORTANT:
----------
- Make sure you open it from iCloud.com in Safari, NOT from Files app
- Once added to home screen, it works like a real app
- Your data persists forever in Safari
